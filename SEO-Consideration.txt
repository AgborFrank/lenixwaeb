To rank `lenixprotocol.com` for recovery, you must adopt the "Forensic/Legal" persona used by CNC Intelligence and Dynamis, rather than just "We recover crypto."

#### **3. Vital Ranking Factors (On-Page Strategy)**

Based on the top competitors, your page **MUST** contain these elements to rank:

1. **"Glass Box" Methodology:**
* *Competitor Secret:* CNC Intelligence and Rexxfield don't just say "we recover." They explain the *process*: **Trace -> Identify -> Subpoena -> Recover**.
* *Action:* Add a "How It Works" section detailing your forensic tools (e.g., "We use heuristics and clustering analysis...").


2. **Trust Signals (Crucial for YMYL - Your Money Your Life):**
* *Competitor Secret:* KeychainX displays a Trustpilot widget. Dynamis displays attorney profiles.
* *Action:* Display "Verified" badges, any certifications (e.g., Chainalysis Reactor Certified), or partnerships with legal firms. If you have a physical office, put the address in the footer; Google favors local verifiable businesses for "Service" queries.


3. **Educational Content Hub:**
* *Competitor Secret:* Rexxfield ranks for "Romance Scams" because they have blog posts describing *how* they happen.
* *Action:* Create a "Scam Database" section on Lenix describing recent scams (e.g., "Pig Butchering Scams," "Fake Exchange Phishing"). This captures traffic from victims searching for the specific scam they fell for.


4. **Distinct Service Areas:**
* Split your offering into two clear buckets:
* **Wallet Recovery:** For people who lost passwords/seeds (like KeychainX).
* **Fraud Investigation:** For people who were scammed (like CNC Intel).
